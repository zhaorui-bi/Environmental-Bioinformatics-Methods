## 课程简介 | Course Description

生物信息学是计算机科学、统计学与生命科学的交叉学科，伴随高通量组学技术的发展而快速成熟。随着基因组学、转录组学、蛋白质组学和代谢组学数据规模持续增长，环境生物学研究对数据挖掘、算法建模与可复现分析流程的需求显著提升。  
Bioinformatics is an interdisciplinary field across computer science, statistics, and life sciences, and it has rapidly evolved with high-throughput omics technologies. As genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics datasets continue to grow, environmental biology increasingly requires data mining, algorithmic modeling, and reproducible analysis workflows.

本课程聚焦生物信息学在环境生物学中的应用，覆盖从分子生物学基础、数据库与序列分析，到群落结构、系统发育与项目设计的完整链条。课程强调“方法可解释、流程可复现、结果可验证”，通过讲授与上机结合，帮助研究生建立独立开展环境生物信息学研究的能力。  
This course focuses on bioinformatics applications in environmental biology, covering molecular biology foundations, databases and sequence analysis, community structure, phylogenetics, and project design. It emphasizes interpretable methods, reproducible workflows, and verifiable results, enabling graduate students to conduct independent environmental bioinformatics research.

## 课程目标 | Learning Objectives

1. 建立环境生物信息学核心概念与方法体系。  
   Build a solid conceptual and methodological framework for environmental bioinformatics.
2. 掌握常见数据格式、数据库检索、序列比对与注释分析流程。  
   Master common data formats, database search, sequence alignment, and annotation workflows.
3. 理解并应用 HMM、系统发育分析、群落结构分析等关键方法。  
   Understand and apply key methods including HMM, phylogenetic analysis, and community-structure analysis.
4. 能够围绕真实环境问题设计并实现可复现的分析流程。  
   Design and implement reproducible pipelines for real environmental research problems.
5. 具备批判性评估工具、结果与研究假设的能力。  
   Critically evaluate tools, results, and underlying research hypotheses.

## 考核方式 | Assessment

- 平时作业与上机练习：25%。  
  Regular homework and lab practice: 25%.
- 研究案例报告（案例 I/II/III，对应第 9、12、14 讲）：30%。  
  Case-study reports (Cases I/II/III in Lectures 9/12/14): 30%.
- 课程项目设计（第 15 讲）：10%。  
  Project proposal/design (Lecture 15): 10%.
- 期末小组报告（第 16 讲，含书面材料与汇报）：25%。  
  Final team report and presentation (Lecture 16): 25%.
- 课堂参与与出勤：10%。  
  Class participation and attendance: 10%.

## 成绩说明 | Grading Notes

- 总评成绩采用百分制，60 分及以上为通过。  
  Final grading uses a 100-point scale; 60 or above is passing.
- 小组作业需提交成员分工说明，支持过程性追踪。  
  Team submissions must include contribution statements for process-level tracking.
- 所有提交物需包含可复现实验信息（环境、版本、命令、输入输出说明）。  
  All submissions must provide reproducibility details (environment, versions, commands, and I/O description).
