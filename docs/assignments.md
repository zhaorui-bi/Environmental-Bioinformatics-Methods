# Assignments

## Design Principles

- Every assignment must be reproducible on a clean machine.
- Submissions include both scientific interpretation and engineering evidence.
- Grading favors clarity, correctness, and robustness over visual polish.

## Assignment Tracks

1. Homework 0: Environment and reproducibility warm-up
2. Homework 1: Similarity search and annotation mini-task
3. Homework 2: Assembly + binning + QC pipeline mini-task
4. Homework 3: Ecological interpretation and reporting mini-task

Detailed templates live in the repository:

- `assignments/README.md`
- `assignments/hw0_environment.md`
- `assignments/hw1_similarity.md`
- `assignments/hw2_metagenome_pipeline.md`
- `assignments/hw3_ecology_reporting.md`

## Standard Submission Layout

```text
team-or-student-id/
├── README.md
├── env/
├── scripts/
├── config/
├── results/
└── report/
```

## Rubric Baseline

- Scientific correctness: 40%
- Reproducibility and engineering quality: 35%
- Interpretation and communication: 25%
